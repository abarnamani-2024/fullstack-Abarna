Parents Guide to School Admissions

Curriculum

Selecting the curriculum is the most important criteria for the parents and the first step in identifying the best International School in Bangalore. Every parent's common question is, 'Which board is best for my child'?. Understanding the child's capacity and interest is of the utmost importance while selecting the curriculum. CBSE and Cambridge IGCSE curricula, both are nowadays popular in India and the syllabus is according to International standards so it is beneficial to students who are looking for higher studies abroad. Also, having the best teachers and support faculties is very much important to implement these syllabuses to the best of their abilities.



School Location and Distance from Home

Location is another factor that parents need to keep in mind while selecting the International School in Bangalore. The top school which is 20-30km away from your home doesn't make sense at all. Travelling 2-3 hours daily will make your child tired especially during traffic or monsoon season. In case of an emergency, it is always better to find a school that is within 10km
